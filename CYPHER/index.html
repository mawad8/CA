How to get started

Make The Call

During your call, we will answer any and all questions you have. At that time we will be happy to scehdule a tour for you to come see our beautiful campus. You will meet with one of our team members and discuss the program in further detail and see if our program is right for you.

Choose Payment Method

The total cost of our program is $2500. If you would prefer to make monthly payments we have many options avaiable. We can help you get a loan if needed. You decide which option suits your situation best. Making the first payment secures your spot in our program. Classes start on the 1st of every month.

Become A Developer

While in our program we will teach you the most current and in-demand computer languages, design, whiteboarding, professional portfolio and site building, and help you to create a professional resume. You will earn a Front-End Web Development Certificate. You will be given a three month internship and help with job placement.

YOUR PATHWAY

Sixteen Weeks To A New Career

  • Step 1

    Secure Your Spot

    Complete your prerequisite coursework. We will give you work to complete that will help you throughout the course and throughout your life as a web developer. Don't worry we will have videos to guide you through the process of how to set your developer toolbox up. We will prepare you to be ready for your first day of the start of your new life.

  • Step 2

    Start Your Sixteen Weeks

    Our teachers will give you the introductions to the languages you will learn. Throughout the courses you will learn very relevant and current computer languages such as React, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and JQUERY. You will learn the best designs for websites and you will also learn whitebaording. We will assist you and guide you to be professional.

  • Step 3

    Becoming a Professional

    During your course you will have completed a professional portfolio with our guidance. You will also have completed a professional resume, cover letter, and everything you need to get hired. We will be working with you to finish all of these steps to build yourself professionally.

  • Step 4

    Preparing to Get Hired

    Our professional team is going to call you to set up an appointment for you to come and prepare for interviews. We will go over all of your projects and resume to make sure that everything is the best it can be before we apply you for jobs. We will sit and talk about what companies are looking for. You will practice for the interview and we will tell you how to get started on this process of getting hired as a web developer.
